Palestine Restaurant ♥♥


A Palestinian grill and breakfast restaurant feature a variety of grilled meats such as lamb, chicken, or beef, cooked with traditional Palestinian spices and marinades. Popular dishes could include shish kebabs, grilled chicken or lamb chops, and mixed grills.

In terms of breakfast, Palestinian cuisine offers a range of delicious options. Traditional breakfast dishes might include falafel, hummus, foul medames (fava beans), labneh (strained yogurt), fresh vegetables, olives, and various types of bread like pita or taboon bread.
